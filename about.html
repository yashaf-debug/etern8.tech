Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Alex Radchenko, Etern8 Tech was born from a vision to combine cutting-edge AI with agile development methodologies. Starting as a two-person team in Moscow, we rapidly grew by delivering hyper-fast, high-impact digital products for startups and enterprises alike.

Within our first year, we launched over 30 chatbots, landing pages, and AI-powered analytics platforms — each in under 6 weeks, average client NPS: 72. Our dedication to speed, reliability and adaptability has made us a trusted partner across Russia, the UAE and Saudi Arabia.

Today, our global team of 15 specialists works remotely and on-site, helping clients scale infinitely without losing human touch.
